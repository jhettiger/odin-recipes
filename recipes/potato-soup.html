Cheesy Loaded Potato Soup

This might take a while but well worth the time!

    Ingredients

  • 3 Russet baking potatoes baked
  • 12 slices of bacon (1 package)
  • 3 green onions
  • 2/3 cup butter
  • 2/3 cup flour
  • 7 cups milk
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 cups cheese (or as much as you want)
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp pepper

    Steps

  1. Cook bacon in a seperate skillet and set aside to be crumbled.
  2. Over medium heat, begin to melt your butter.
  3. Once butter has melted, add flour and stir until mixed.
  4. Slowly begin to add milk, stirring to thicken.
  5. Add chopped potatoes and green onions and bring to a boil.
  6. Once boiling, reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  7. After the soup has simmered, mix in crumbled bacon, cheese, sour cream, salt and peper.
  8. Stir until cheese has melted and everything has completely mixed together.
  9. Enjoy!
