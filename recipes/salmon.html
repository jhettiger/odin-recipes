Lemon Butter Salmon with Garlic

This is such an easy recipe for amazing salmon!

    Ingredients

  • 1 Salmon Center Cut fillet
  • 1/4 cup butter (melted)
  • 1/4 fresh squeezed lemon juice
  • 3 cloves garlic minced
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste

    Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Cut salmon fillet into 3 equal portions
  3. Cover salmon with your garlic
  4. Mix the melted butter and lemon juice and pour over salmon
  5. Salt and pepper to your liking
  6. Place salmon in the oven and let cook for 8 minutes
  7. After the salmon has cooked for 8 minutes, finish cooking by broiling for 2 minutes or until the salmon is cooked to your liking.
