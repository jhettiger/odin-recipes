Lemon Garlic Green Beans

My wife loves these but I have never tried them!

    Ingredients

  • 1 lb green beans halved
  • 4 cloves garlic minced
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Juice from half a lemon

    Steps

  1. Snap off both ends of green beans and snap green beans in half.
  2. In a skillet, cover green beans with water and bring to a boil.
  3. While bringing to a boil, start to cut up and prepare garlic.
  4. Once boiling, reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Drain water from the skillet, and add your butter.
  6. Cook over medium heat for about 5-10 minutes.
  7. Add in minced garlic, and a little bit of lemon juice.
  8. Cook for another 10 minutes, or until green beans are done to your liking.
  9. Once done, add salt and pepper and enjoy!
